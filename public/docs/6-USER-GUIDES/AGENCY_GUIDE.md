# Agency Guide

Comprehensive guide for agency owners managing multiple client accounts, campaigns, and team members.

---

## Your Dashboard

As an agency owner, your **Dashboard** shows:
- Total active clients
- Campaigns across all clients
- Recent activity and alerts
- Revenue and credit balance

---

## Client Management

### Navigating to Client Management

1. Click **Agency** → **Client Management** in the sidebar

### Creating a New Client

1. Click **Add Client**
2. Enter client information:
   - Company name
   - Contact name and email
   - Industry type
   - Phone number
3. Configure initial settings:
   - Credit allocation (if sharing agency credits)
   - Default notification preferences
4. Click **Create Client**

### Managing Existing Clients

From the Client Management list:
- **View Details**: Click on a client to see their profile and campaigns
- **Edit**: Update client information
- **Switch Context**: Impersonate client to manage their campaigns
- **Deactivate**: Temporarily disable client access

### Client Context Switching

To work within a client's account:
1. Go to **Agency** → **Client Management**
2. Click on the client name
3. Use the **Switch to Client** button
4. You'll now see the platform from the client's perspective
5. Return to agency view using the breadcrumb or agency dropdown

---

## Campaign Management for Clients

### Creating Campaigns for Clients

1. Switch to the client's context (or select client in campaign wizard)
2. Navigate to **Campaigns** → **All Campaigns**
3. Click **Create Campaign**
4. Follow the campaign wizard:
   - **Step 1**: Campaign name and details
   - **Step 2**: Select recipients from client's contacts
   - **Step 3**: Choose mail template
   - **Step 4**: Configure landing page
   - **Step 5**: Set up tracking and rewards
   - **Step 6**: Review and submit

### Viewing All Client Campaigns

From your agency dashboard, you can see campaigns across all clients:
- Filter by client
- Filter by campaign status
- Sort by date, performance, or spend
- Export campaign reports

### Campaign Approval Workflow

If your agency uses approval workflows:
1. Clients submit campaigns for review
2. You receive notification of pending campaigns
3. Review campaign details
4. Approve to send to production, or request changes

## Managing Client Contacts

### Accessing Client Contacts

1. Switch to client context
2. Navigate to **Audience** → **Contacts**

### Importing Contacts for Clients

1. **Audience** → **Import Contacts**
2. Upload CSV file
3. Map columns to fields
4. Assign to client
5. Confirm import

### Creating Contact Lists

1. **Audience** → **Lists & Segments**
2. Click **Create List**
3. Choose list type:
   - **Static**: Manually selected contacts
   - **Dynamic Segment**: Contacts matching filter criteria
4. Configure and save

## Gift Card Management

### Navigating to Gift Cards

1. Click **Rewards** → **Gift Card Inventory** in the sidebar

### Managing Inventory for Clients

- View gift card inventory available for campaigns
- Monitor usage across client campaigns
- Track provisioning and delivery status

### Credits & Billing

1. Click **Rewards** → **Credits & Billing**
2. View credit balance
3. Purchase additional credits
4. Configure auto-reload settings
5. View transaction history

### Allocating Credits to Clients

If you manage credits for clients:
- Set credit limits per client
- Monitor client spend
- Top up client credits as needed

## Mail Library

### Navigating to Mail Library

1. Click **Campaigns** → **Mail Library** in the sidebar

### Managing Templates for Clients

- Create template designs for client use
- Import agency-standard templates
- Lock templates clients can't modify
- Share templates across clients

### Creating a New Template

1. Click **Create New**
2. Select template size (4x6, 6x9, etc.)
3. Use the visual designer or upload artwork
4. Add merge fields for personalization
5. Save and assign to client(s)

## Team Management

### Managing Agency Team Members

1. Navigate to **Workspace** → **Team**
2. View all team members in your agency

### Inviting Team Members

1. Click **Invite Team Member**
2. Enter email address
3. Select role:
   - **Agency Owner**: Full agency access
   - **Account Manager**: Manages assigned clients
   - **Designer**: Creates templates and landing pages
4. Assign client permissions
5. Send invitation

### Role Assignments

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| Agency Owner | All clients, all features |
| Account Manager | Assigned clients only |
| Designer | Template creation, no client data |
| Support | Read-only access for support |

## Reporting

### Agency-Wide Reports

View aggregate reporting across all clients:
- Total mail volume
- Response rates by client
- Gift card usage
- Revenue metrics

### Generating Reports

1. Navigate to dashboard or specific campaign
2. Click **Export Report**
3. Select format (PDF, CSV, Excel)
4. Choose data range and metrics
5. Download or email report

### Client-Facing Reports

Generate white-labeled reports for clients:
- Campaign performance summaries
- ROI analysis
- Response attribution
- Recommendations

---

## Billing Management

### Viewing Agency Billing

1. **Rewards** → **Credits & Billing**
2. View credit balance and purchase history
3. Download invoices

### Client Billing Options

Configure how clients are billed:
- **Agency-Funded**: Agency pays for all campaigns
- **Client-Funded**: Clients purchase their own credits
- **Hybrid**: Agency provides credits, clients pay overages

## Best Practices

### Client Onboarding

1. **Create client profile** with complete information
2. **Import initial contacts** from client's existing database
3. **Set up templates** with client branding
4. **Configure first campaign** as a guided demo
5. **Train client users** on platform features

### Campaign Management

1. **Standardize workflows**: Use consistent campaign templates
2. **Review before submission**: Always verify campaign details
3. **Monitor performance**: Check campaign metrics daily
4. **Communicate results**: Regular client updates

### Quality Control

1. **Proofread everything**: Mail pieces, landing pages, SMS messages
2. **Verify data**: Ensure contact lists are clean
3. **Test tracking**: Confirm PURLs and QR codes work
4. **Check inventory**: Ensure gift cards are available

### Client Communication

1. **Weekly updates** on active campaigns
2. **Monthly performance reviews**
3. **Quarterly strategy sessions**
4. **Immediate alerts** for issues
