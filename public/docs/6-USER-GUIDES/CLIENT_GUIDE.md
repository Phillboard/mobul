# Client Guide

Comprehensive guide for clients viewing campaigns, managing contacts, and tracking results.

---

## Your Dashboard

As a client, your **Dashboard** shows:
- Active campaign status
- Recent responses and conversions
- Gift card redemption activity
- Key performance metrics

---

## Viewing Your Campaigns

### Navigating to Campaigns

1. Click **Campaigns** → **All Campaigns** in the sidebar

### Campaign List View

Your campaigns are displayed with:
- Campaign name
- Status (Draft, Active, Completed)
- Mail count
- Response rate
- Date created/launched

### Campaign Details

Click on any campaign to see:
- **Overview**: Campaign summary and KPIs
- **Recipients**: List of people who received mail
- **Responses**: Landing page visits, form submissions, calls
- **Gift Cards**: Redemption status and delivery
- **Analytics**: Detailed performance metrics

### Campaign Status Meanings

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Draft** | Being configured, not yet submitted |
| **In Production** | Submitted to print vendor |
| **Mailed** | Mail pieces sent via USPS |
| **Active** | Campaign running, tracking responses |
| **Completed** | Campaign finished |

## Managing Your Contacts

### Navigating to Contacts

1. Click **Audience** → **Contacts** in the sidebar

### Viewing Contacts

Your contact database shows:
- Name and contact information
- Tags and segments
- Campaign history
- Engagement score

### Contact Details

Click on any contact to see:
- Full profile information
- Campaign participation history
- All interactions (visits, calls, emails)
- Gift card redemptions
- Notes and tags

---

## Importing Contacts

### Navigating to Import

1. Click **Audience** → **Import Contacts** in the sidebar

### Import Steps

1. **Upload File**: Drag and drop or select a CSV file
2. **Map Columns**: Match CSV columns to contact fields
3. **Review**: Check the preview for errors
4. **Confirm**: Complete the import

### Required Fields

- First Name or Full Name
- Address
- City
- State
- ZIP Code

### Optional Fields

- Phone number
- Email address
- Custom tags
- Additional data fields

### Handling Duplicates

During import, the system will:
- Identify existing contacts by email or phone
- Update existing records with new data
- Flag potential duplicates for review

## Lists & Segments

### Navigating to Lists

1. Click **Audience** → **Lists & Segments** in the sidebar

### Creating a Static List

1. Click **Create List**
2. Select **Static List**
3. Name your list
4. Add contacts manually or from search results
5. Save

### Creating a Dynamic Segment

1. Click **Create List**
2. Select **Dynamic Segment**
3. Name your segment
4. Configure filter rules:
   - Demographics (city, state, ZIP)
   - Engagement (last interaction, score)
   - Tags and attributes
   - Campaign history
5. Save

Dynamic segments automatically update as contacts match or unmatch criteria.

## Understanding Analytics

### Campaign Metrics

| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Delivery Rate** | Percentage of mail successfully delivered |
| **Response Rate** | Percentage who visited landing page or called |
| **Conversion Rate** | Percentage who completed desired action |
| **Gift Card Rate** | Percentage who received gift cards |
| **Cost Per Lead** | Total cost divided by leads generated |

### Reading the Dashboard

- **Trend Lines**: Show performance over time
- **Comparisons**: Compare to previous campaigns
- **Benchmarks**: Industry averages for reference

### Exporting Data

1. Navigate to campaign or contacts
2. Click **Export** button
3. Select format (CSV, Excel, PDF)
4. Choose data to include
5. Download file

## Best Practices

### Contact Data Quality

1. **Use verified addresses**: Ensure mailing addresses are deliverable
2. **Keep data current**: Update contacts regularly
3. **Remove duplicates**: Clean your database periodically
4. **Add context**: Use tags and notes for better segmentation

### Campaign Performance

1. **Start small**: Test with 100-500 contacts first
2. **Monitor daily**: Check metrics during active campaigns
3. **Act quickly**: Follow up on leads within 24 hours
4. **Learn and iterate**: Apply learnings to next campaign

### Response Handling

1. **Prepare for calls**: Have staff ready to answer
2. **Track outcomes**: Note what happens with each lead
3. **Close the loop**: Update contact records with results

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I know if my mail was delivered?

Campaign analytics show delivery tracking via USPS Intelligent Mail barcodes. Check the **Delivery** section of your campaign.

### Why didn't someone receive their gift card?

Check the recipient's status in the campaign. Common reasons:
- They didn't meet the reward condition
- Phone/email was invalid
- SMS delivery failed (check with support)

### Can I change a campaign after it's submitted?

Once a campaign is in production, changes are limited. Contact your agency or support for assistance.

### How long until I see results?

- Mail typically arrives 3-7 days after mailing
- Responses usually peak 5-10 days after first delivery
- Complete results available 2-3 weeks after mailing
